WeConnect

The FPO(farmers producer organisation) promotes the growth of farmers in terms of governance and finance. But many of the farmers have difficulty in understanding the concept of FPO and face lots of problem with legalization, and financial and governance activity because they don’t have the proper knowledge or experience. Also, a lot of FPOs don’t have proper consultancy support to understand their activities or producers.



The WeConnect platform, helps the farmers to know more about how they can improve their capacity of production by providing various resources to learn more about schemes, marketing, and reaching out to more customers by providing a market place to list products and set the timeline of their availability. The platform also provides features like bidding, SMS, forum, and multilingual translator.
