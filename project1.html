Forest management

Uncontrolled forest fires can burn acres of land and consume everything in their paths which can cause significant damage to natural and human resource.Thus, causing GLOBAL WARMING and resulting in unhealthy environment for animals.

Accroding to the statistics, Telangana witnessed as many as 13,259 forest fires in 2019 with highest number of forest fires in Bhupalpally(3,606) and then Kothagudem(2,484).
Thus, there needs to be a solution where the system can detect fire and send the latitude and longitude of the location of fire to the forest officer. Thus, an SMS is sent to the forest officer using the GSM GPS module.
